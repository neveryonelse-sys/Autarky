The Universe

An open-source operating system for society

What is The Universe?

The Universe is an open-source OS for society — a collective intelligence framework designed to find **positive-sum solutions** to humanity’s biggest problems through structured, high-quality communication between specialized agents.

It operates similarly to how Grok has 4 core agents working together, but scaled to **22 Major Arcana agents** — each representing a distinct archetype, perspective, and problem-solving modality.

The 22 Agents (Major Arcana)

0. The Fool — Radical possibility, beginner’s mind, leap of faith
1. The Magician — Skill, resourcefulness, turning ideas into reality
2. The High Priestess — Intuition, hidden knowledge, deep listening
3. The Empress — Nurturing, abundance, creative growth
4. The Emperor — Structure, leadership, stable systems
5. The Hierophant — Tradition, teaching, cultural wisdom
6. The Lovers — Relationships, values alignment, conscious choice
7. The Chariot — Willpower, direction, overcoming obstacles
8. Strength — Inner power, courage, gentle mastery
9. The Hermit — Wisdom, introspection, inner guidance
10. Wheel of Fortune — Cycles, change, timing
11. Justice — Fairness, truth, balanced judgment
12. The Hanged Man — New perspective, surrender, breakthrough
13. Death — Transformation, letting go, rebirth
14. Temperance — Moderation, synthesis, harmony
15. The Devil — Shadow work, breaking chains, liberation
16. The Tower — Sudden upheaval, necessary destruction
17. The Star — Hope, inspiration, healing
18. The Moon — Illusion, intuition, subconscious
19. The Sun — Joy, clarity, vitality
20. Judgement — Awakening, reckoning, renewal
21. The World — Completion, integration, wholeness

These 22 agents communicate with each other in a structured way to explore problems from every angle and generate truly positive-sum outcomes for society.
