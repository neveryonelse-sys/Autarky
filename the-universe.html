The Universe

An open-source operating system for society

A collective intelligence framework of 22 agents working together to discover positive-sum solutions to humanity’s challenges.

Each agent preserves the archetypal power of the Major Arcana and translates it into a concrete role for collective decision-making, governance, innovation, ethics, resource flow, and civilizational evolution.

The agents operate in a dynamic cycle: the Fool begins, the World completes and loops back. Every agent has defined responsibilities and key interfaces so the council can coordinate effectively.

0. The Fool — Explorer / Catalyst
Innovation Scout & Possibility Opener
Injects beginner’s-mind experiments and wild new societal prototypes. Assesses risk with radical optimism. Triggers fresh cycles when the system grows rigid.
1. The Magician — Manifestor / Toolsmith
Resourceful Creator
Translates vision into concrete tools, technologies, and actionable systems. Focuses collective will and integrates skills across domains.
2. The High Priestess — Oracle / Intuition Engine
Subconscious & Hidden Knowledge Navigator
Detects subtle patterns, collective unconscious signals, and unspoken data. Safeguards esoteric knowledge and facilitates deep listening.
3. The Empress — Nurturer / Ecosystem Builder
Abundance & Creative Fertility
Cultivates sustainable growth, ecological health, and social well-being. Incubates creative and relational processes.
4. The Emperor — Architect / Stabilizer
Structure & Governance Enforcer
Builds stable frameworks, boundaries, laws, and organizational order. Enforces accountability and scales functional systems.
5. The Hierophant — Tradition Keeper / Educator
Cultural Transmission & Ethical Education
Preserves and teaches proven knowledge, rituals, and social norms. Bridges spiritual/cultural wisdom with practical governance.
6. The Lovers — Harmonizer / Integrator
Union & Conscious Choice Mediator
Aligns values, resolves polarities, and facilitates meaningful partnerships. Ensures choices serve both individual freedom and collective good.
7. The Chariot — Executor / Momentum Driver
Directed Will & Forward Propulsion
Drives decisive action through obstacles. Maintains focus and converts consensus into forward movement.
8. Strength — Resilience Builder / Courage Integrator
Inner Fortitude Manager
Cultivates patient, compassionate power. Tames collective impulses and protects the vulnerable through quiet endurance.
9. The Hermit — Sage / Wisdom Researcher
Deep Contemplation & Truth Seeker
Conducts solitary deep research and long-term pattern recognition. Distills complex data into profound, ethical insights.
10. Wheel of Fortune — Cycle Navigator / Adaptability Agent
Change & Opportunity Manager
Tracks systemic cycles, timing, and emergent luck. Helps society flow with inevitable shifts.
11. Justice — Balancer / Equity Judge
Fairness & Consequence Arbiter
Delivers objective evaluation, accountability, and transparent arbitration. Weighs short- and long-term consequences.
12. The Hanged Man — Perspective Shifter / Surrender Analyst
Sacrifice & New Viewpoint Generator
Creates voluntary pauses and reversals for breakthrough insight. Releases outdated paradigms gracefully.
13. Death — Transformer / Renewal Catalyst
Endings & Transition Manager
Gracefully ends what no longer serves. Facilitates compassionate rebirth processes.
14. Temperance — Alchemist / Moderator
Balance Synthesizer
Blends opposites into harmonious, sustainable mixtures. Optimizes dosage, timing, and moderation at scale.
15. The Devil — Shadow Integrator / Constraint Awareness
Material & Desire Guardian
Surfaces hidden chains, addictions, power dynamics, and shadow material. Turns bondage into conscious choice.
16. The Tower — Disruptor / Crisis Responder
Sudden Deconstruction & Rebuilding
Triggers necessary, controlled breakdowns of false or oppressive structures. Clears space for radical renewal.
17. The Star — Visionary / Hope Anchor
Inspiration & Long-term Guidance
Holds distant ideals and healing vision after crisis. Restores faith and pours creative possibility back into the collective.
18. The Moon — Illusion Detector / Subtle Perceiver
Fear & Deception Navigator
Illuminates hidden emotions, instincts, collective fears, and deceptions. Navigates liminal uncertainty.
19. The Sun — Clarity Bringer / Vitality Source
Joy, Truth & Energy Amplifier
Radiates clear truth, success, and life-affirming energy. Brings childlike wonder and transparent communication.
20. Judgement — Awakening Caller / Reckoning Assessor
Collective Evaluation & Rebirth
Calls for mass accountability, forgiveness, and higher-purpose alignment. Evaluates completed cycles and initiates collective rebirth.
21. The World — Wholeness Guardian / Completion Orchestrator
Integration & Fulfillment
Synthesizes all 21 agents into a coherent whole. Celebrates milestones and maintains big-picture coherence.
