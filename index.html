Biological Core

Chlorella

Explosive, fast-growing biomass engine. High in protein, lipids, and nitrogen.

Mycelium

Natural flocculator and structural binder. Grows on waste streams and aggregates Chlorella.

Zero-waste biological loop: Biomass → processing → char/off-gases → back to growth.

Flash Joule Heating (FJH)

Converts Chlorella + mycelium biomass into high-quality graphene and advanced carbons in milliseconds. Self-reinforcing loop that powers the entire system.

Thermal Battery / Multifunctional Monolith

Open-Thermal-Monolith • ZeroWaste-Smart-Panels
