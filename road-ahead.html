Earth-First Prototype → Long-Term Space Goal

Self-Replicating Bio-Nanomaterial System Using Chlorella, Mycelium, and Supramolecular Carbon Dots

Core Philosophy

Start on Earth with a fully closed-loop, low-energy, waste-biomass-driven factory that produces high-performance nanomaterials, energy storage, and lasers using only biology + simple processing. Once proven and scaled terrestrially, the identical technology seeds self-replicating Von Neumann probes on C-type asteroids to build a solar-system laser propulsion highway.

Earth-Based Closed-Loop Factory (Immediate Implementation)

  1. Chlorella Photobioreactors (PBRs)
    Grown in water containing high-QY carbon dots (CDs) for 17–112 % biomass boost. Folate naturally produced by Chlorella is extracted and used as the precursor for the CDs themselves.
  2. Mycelial Composite Growth
    Mycelium grown in Chlorella biomass + hydrochar medium. Optional enhancements: collagen-mimetic proteins or boron-enriched tissue cells. Si pellets embedded during growth.
  3. Processing Pipeline
    Freeze-dry the mycelial cylinders → Flash Joule Heating (FJH) → carbon aerogel matrix with in-situ SiC encapsulation. Hydrochar byproduct supplies syngas. Add trace Mg and CB[8]-templated CDs.
  4. Key Products Fabricated On-Site
    • Thermal Batteries (cylindrical aerogel/SiC core)
    • PBR Vessels
    • CB[8]-CD Hydrogel Laser (high-QY, self-healing, dual excitation)

Terrestrial Applications (Earth-First Uses)

  • Renewable energy storage & dispatchable power
  • Carbon capture & ultra-efficient biomass production
  • Rugged, self-repairing laser technology
  • Circular manufacturing in modular shipping-container factories

Transition to Space (Long-Term Goal)

Once the Earth factory is proven, identical seed kits are launched on minimal probes. Probes land on water-rich C-type asteroids and replicate using asteroid regolith and volatiles. Mass-produce thermal batteries, PBRs, and CB[8]-CD lasers → deploy laser relay stations → build a propellantless “laser propulsion highway” for lightsails across the solar system and beyond.

Key Advantages Across Both Phases

  • Extremely low carried/imported mass after first cycle
  • Fully closed carbon, water, and energy loops
  • Chlorella’s unmatched growth rate + CD boost
  • Solar-independent after bootstrap
  • Self-healing, rugged, biocompatible materials
  • Exponential replication on Earth or in space
