# Autarky sCO₂ Turbine-Generator – Current Design (Advanced Version)

**Document Date:** May 17, 2026  
**Status:** Linchpin component of Autarky – the high-power-density generator that powers the vehicle, house, seed-ship, and replication swarm.  
**Design Philosophy:** 99 %+ abundant-elements (silicon, carbon, oxygen, titanium, boron), self-repairable via the Metastellar Forge, Tower-gated for high-energy safety, and built to last decades while maintaining high power density.

## 1. Overview
The Autarky turbine-generator is a compact, high-efficiency **supercritical CO₂ (sCO₂) Brayton-cycle machine** with an **integrated axial-flux generator** built directly into the turbomachinery. It converts heat from the molten-silicon thermal battery into electrical power at 45–65 % thermal-to-electric efficiency.

Key design goals:
- Extreme power density (target 800–2,000+ kW in a desk-sized or “power-spine” package).
- Zero or near-zero rare-earth magnets (replaced by twisted-graphene + boron/MT-CD pseudo-magnets).
- Self-repair and continuous upgrading via the Metastellar Forge and household FJH.
- Seamless integration with the silicon thermal battery, sCO₂ HVAC loop, regenerative suspension, and re-entry charging.

It is the true **linchpin** of Autarky: once this unit exists and can be rebuilt from waste graphene, the rest of the system (vehicles, houses, seed-ships) becomes dramatically easier.

## 2. Physical Architecture – “Power Spine”
- **Form factor**: Dual (or single modular) long-and-skinny contra-rotating shafts running along the vehicle chassis or house basement wall (“power spine”).
  - Length: 1.5–3 m  
  - Diameter: 15–25 cm  
  - 8–12 interleaved axial-flow stages.
- **Contra-rotation**: Two shafts spin in opposite directions at 20,000–75,000 RPM. This doubles relative speed across every rotor-stator gap, dramatically increasing torque and power density without enlarging the unit.
- **Integrated design**: Turbine blades sit on the outer annulus of each rotor disk; the inner annulus contains the electrical/magnetic sections. No separate generator housing — the turbomachinery *is* the generator.

## 3. Magnetic Field System (Pseudo-Magnet Core)
- **Primary field source**: Twisted bilayer/trilayer graphene sheets grown at magic-angle precision (≈1.1° or low-angle regimes) using the Metastellar Forge.
- **Enhancement**: Boron doping or thin hBN interfacial layers + melatonin-derived carbon dots (MT-CDs) for photo-tunable modulation (blackbody light from the thermal battery can adjust field strength on demand).
- **Effect**: Generates pseudo-magnetic fields up to hundreds of Tesla locally — far stronger per unit mass than conventional rare-earth magnets.
- **Rare-earth policy**: Any residual rare-earth atoms are lidar-tracked and minimized each Forge cycle. The design evolves toward fully magnet-free operation.

## 4. Electrical System
- **Coils & current collectors**: Multi-layer graphene (later MXene-graphene hybrids) printed or deposited on the interleaved rotor/stator disks.
- **Air gap**: Minimal 0.1–0.3 mm (advanced target), maintained by compliant carbon/silica aerogel bump foils.
- **Topology**: Multi-stator multi-rotor (MSMR) axial-flux configuration. Contra-rotation + minimal gap yields extremely high torque density.
- **Output**: High-frequency AC that feeds directly into graphene IPT coils for in-wheel motors (vehicle) or the house DC bus.

## 5. Fluid & Cooling System
- **Working fluid**: Supercritical CO₂ (same closed loop as the rest of Autarky).
- **Flow control**: Precision micronozzles stage expansion and provide active cooling exactly where eddy-current heat builds.
- **Bearings**: GO-LIPSS laser-textured foil bearings (self-lubricating, oil-free). sCO₂ itself forms the hydrodynamic film.
- **Thermal management**: Integrated thin TEG arrays and carbon-dot-tuned TPV layers between stator disks harvest waste heat. MXene heat-spreader films further improve dissipation.

## 6. Integration with the Rest of Autarky
- **Thermal battery**: Heat from the molten-silicon modules (pre-heated by re-entry plasma on spacecraft) drives the sCO₂ cycle. Blackbody radiation through silica-aerogel windows feeds TPV/TEG harvest directly into the generator.
- **Metastellar Forge & FJH**: Every component (rotors, stators, bearings, coils) is modular and Forge-replaceable. The generator’s own waste heat can drive a side-loop Forge cycle to print its own spare parts from household waste.
- **Vehicle / house**: The “power spine” fits under the vehicle floor or along a basement wall. Excess power keeps the silicon batteries hot and feeds the FJH appliance.
- **Re-entry charging**: On seed-ships, atmospheric plasma heat charges the thermal batteries while the generator remains ready for immediate post-landing use.

## 7. Power Density & Performance Targets
- **Current baseline**: 400–1,200 kW electrical in a desk-sized package.
- **Advanced target (with MXene-graphene, pseudo-magnets, minimal gap, and multi-stage stacking)**: 800–2,000+ kW in the same physical envelope.
- **Efficiency**: 45–65 % thermal-to-electric (including TEG/TPV recovery).
- **Longevity**: Designed for decades of service life with periodic Forge-printed upgrades. Self-healing GO-LIPSS surfaces and aerogel compliance ensure graceful degradation.
