Thermal Battery / Multifunctional Monolith

The self-replicating heart of Autarky — heat storage, power generation, laser production, structural material, and more.

Core Composition & Self-Replication

The monolith is created in a single Flash Joule Heating (FJH) step from mycelium/chlorella/hydrochar biomass + Mg/Si precursors. It forms a robust SiC shell containing a molten silicon core that stores enormous thermal energy at high temperature with a stable latent-heat plateau.

Multifunctional Capabilities

  • Thermal Battery — Stores heat for hours with exceptional stability, powering TPVs and other processes.
  • Integrated Laser Face — The non-TPV face functions as a high-gain, self-pumped random laser with diamond Raman beam cleanup for self-replication and fabrication.
  • Thermoacoustic Coupling — The monolith can directly drive thermoacoustic chambers, converting stored heat into acoustic waves for additional electricity generation, cooling, or heat pumping without moving parts.
  • Support for PBRs — Provides stable heat, CO₂, and structural scaffolding for Chlorella photobioreactors, enabling efficient biomass production even in harsh environments.
  • Structural Elements — Grown mycelium-aerogel composites give the monolith exceptional strength-to-weight ratio, making it ideal for habitats, vehicles, and large-scale construction.
  • Air/Water Purification & Remediation — Integrated photocatalysis and mycelium networks actively clean air and water while the monolith operates.

Closed-Loop Self-Replication

The monolith uses its own laser output and excess heat to sinter new preforms, write electrodes, and fabricate additional monoliths. Everything — from biological feedstock to structural components — is produced on-site from abundant local waste.

The Multifunctional Monolith is the literal heart of Autarky: a single unit that stores energy, generates power, fabricates its own successors, supports life systems, and serves as structural building blocks — all in one self-replicating package.
