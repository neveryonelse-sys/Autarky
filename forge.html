Metastellar Forge

Later-Stage Self-Bootstrapping Synthesis Engine

Overview

The Metastellar Forge is an iterative, self-bootstrapping High-Temperature High-Pressure (HTHP) device that upgrades its own containment with every cycle. It represents the crowning synthesis capability of the Autarky system.

Key Capabilities

  • Programmable Supramolecular Templating using CB[8] + different carbon dots (CDs) for precise hierarchical, doped metamaterials.
  • Multi-Physics Synthesis: lasers, microwaves (standing waves), ultrasound, THz, electric/magnetic fields, eventual z-pinch.
  • Zoned & Selective Processing: Protective outer layers and selective scCO₂ allow delicate inner components to survive extreme conditions.
  • Self-Upgrading Containment: Each cycle improves the Forge's own materials and structure.

Inputs & Process

Layered precursors including:

  • Chlorella / mycelium biomass
  • Carbon dots (CDs)
  • PDA (polydopamine)
  • CB[8] + tailored carbon dots for programmable supramolecular assembly

Unique Features

AI-Guided “Songs”

Musicians can map creative parameters to synthesis conditions using a Melda-style VST interface (MSoundFactory / MXXX inspired). Sound design directly influences material creation.

Metamaterial Creation

Produces ultra-high-performance hierarchical metamaterials, including defensive shelters capable of withstanding nuclear-level events, extreme weather, impacts, and radiation.
