Planetary Remediation

Healing the Earth with the same closed-loop tools that build abundance.

Active Healing of a Damaged Planet

Remediation in Autarky is not passive cleanup — it is an active, regenerative process that turns polluted air, water, and soil into resources while restoring ecosystems. Every tool in the system contributes to remediation, creating a virtuous cycle where healing the planet also builds more monoliths, energy, and materials.

Core Remediation Technologies

Carbon Dots (CDs) + Photo- and Piezocatalysis

Carbon dots produced via Flash Joule Heating are highly efficient photocatalysts. Under sunlight they generate reactive oxygen species that break down persistent organic pollutants, microplastics, and toxins. When combined with piezoelectric materials, they also work in the dark or under mechanical stress (flowing water, vibration), enabling 24/7 remediation.

Flash Joule Heating (FJH) & Hydrothermal Carbonization (HTC)

FJH and HTC turn contaminated biomass and waste into hydrochar and advanced carbons. The resulting hydrochar acts as a powerful adsorbent and soil amendment, binding heavy metals and toxins while improving soil structure and microbial health.

Mycelium & Microbes — The Living Remediation Engine

Mycelium and symbiotic microbes are the biological frontline. Their enzymatic networks break down complex pollutants, bind heavy metals, and transform contaminated matter into usable biomass. Mycelium’s fractal structure gives it enormous surface area for filtration and nutrient exchange, making it an incredibly effective bioremediation agent.

Aerogels & Reduction/Ionization Processes

Carbon aerogels (grown with mycelium scaffolds) provide ultra-high surface area filtration and adsorption. Combined with electrochemical reduction and ionization steps powered by the monolith, they selectively remove or neutralize contaminants at the ionic level — turning toxic waste into harmless or even valuable compounds.

Closed-Loop Integration

All remediation outputs feed back into the system: cleaned water irrigates Chlorella PBRs, purified air improves biological efficiency, and recovered materials go straight into new monoliths. The same tools that heal the planet also accelerate abundance creation.

Remediation is therefore not a cost — it is the engine that restores planetary health while powering the self-replicating Autarky infrastructure.
