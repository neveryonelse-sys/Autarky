Energy Architecture

Graphene and Silicon Thermophotovoltaic (TPV) systems powered by the Multifunctional Thermal Monolith

Simple, Scalable, High-Performance Power

The primary energy source in Autarky is **Graphene + Silicon Thermophotovoltaic (TPV) systems** directly driven by the high-temperature blackbody radiation of the Multifunctional Thermal Monolith.

This approach is significantly simpler than traditional turbines while delivering comparable (or superior) power output. The monolith’s stable high-temperature core provides continuous, intense broadband radiation that the TPVs convert efficiently into electricity.

Why Graphene + Si TPVs?

  • No moving parts — extremely reliable and low-maintenance
  • Uses abundant materials (graphene from biomass via FJH, silicon from regolith)
  • High conversion efficiency at the monolith’s operating temperatures
  • Scalable from small modules to large arrays
  • Integrates seamlessly with the monolith’s thermal battery function

The result is a compact, self-contained, zero-maintenance power system that is much easier to bootstrap and replicate than turbine-based designs.
